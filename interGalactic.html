Inter-Galactic was made using only html, javascript and css.

The goal is to survive as long as you can, dodging or shooting the waves of asteroids coming your way.

You start off on Level 1 must survive for the time shown at the top to get to the next level.

Every time you shoot an asteroid, your time will go down.

The game also includes a garage where you can unlock different spaceships as you progress through the levels.
